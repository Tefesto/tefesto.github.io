## Анотація

*Wolfs* (2024) is a crime-comedy film directed by Jon Watts, featuring an ensemble cast led by George Clooney and Brad Pitt. The story follows two fixers, hired to clean up a messy situation involving a young man (Austin Abrams), who inadvertently becomes entangled with a dangerous drug deal. Set in New York City, the film unfolds with a mix of tense situations and humor as the fixers, along with the young man, navigate a chaotic web of dangerous criminals, including Albanians and a Croatian boss.

The movie has elements of a fast-paced crime caper, with witty banter and a chase sequence through the city, but it also explores themes of morality and the unexpected bonds formed in the heat of a crisis. The narrative is peppered with twists, including the revelation that the entire situation may have been orchestrated by a powerful drug dealer aiming to eliminate his rivals. 

In the end, *Wolfs* blends humor and violence, with a strong focus on the dynamics between the two main fixers and the Kid, creating an engaging, if somewhat cartoonish, crime thriller.
